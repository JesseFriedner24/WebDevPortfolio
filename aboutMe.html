A little about me

Greetings! I'm Jesse, a Web Developer living in the Finest City in the World, San Diego. I was born in 1992, in the small desert town of Palm Springs, CA. The summer of 2016 I made the move down to San Diego to gain new opportunities in the tech world. Some of my hobbies include: Racing Motocross, Wakeboarding, Snowboarding, Hiking, Exploring, Going to concerts, Reading, and Photography.

Some History Here's to the past!

Primarily a self-taught developer, I am highly motivated and love learning new technologies. Right after high school I was hired immediately as an automotive technician. During the time at my past career, I knew I wanted to make a change and begin a career that I am passionate about. Creating, building, and fixing have always been passions of mine. With all of the coding languages/frameworks out there, the opportunities to build awesome software using my creativity are endless. I graduated from Origin Code Academy December 16, 2016. The skills I gained from my time there have grown me as a developer tremendously, I now have a great understanding of the Full Lifecycle of Software Development.

Front-End side of Development My passion.

With a passion towards Front-End Development and web Design, I take pride in creating an outstanding visual experience for a customer or user. Seeing an application come together from the design, to the functionality is one of the most gratifying experiences. The ability to work alone or in a group is also something that I love about Web Development. I can fit right into a group for a mob programming session, or happily work alone and zone into the code. What currently motivates me is joining a company that I can contribute to immediately, and be able to grow with as a developer in the long term.
